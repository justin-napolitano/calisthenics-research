# Agent Operating Rules

This repository is a personal calisthenics research and training log. Treat it as a research and planning repo first, not as a medical record system or a coaching authority.

## Boundaries

- Do not present repo content as medical advice.
- Do not prescribe through sharp pain, chest pain, dizziness, faintness, unusual shortness of breath, or injury symptoms.
- If a change depends on injury history, diagnosis, medication, or chronic disease status, mark it as a question for a clinician instead of guessing.
- Preserve raw logs. Corrections should be additive unless the user explicitly asks to rewrite a mistaken entry.
- Keep routines conservative, measurable, and easy to scale.

## Evidence Standard

- Prefer government, clinical, professional society, textbook, or peer-reviewed sources for claims.
- Link claims to source records in `records/sources.yaml` or `docs/research/claim-ledger.md`.
- Separate claims, assumptions, and personal observations.
- When updating a routine, say what changed and why.

## File Conventions

- Daily logs go in `logs/YYYY-MM-DD.md`.
- Research source notes go in `docs/research/YYYY-MM-DD-topic.md`.
- Human-readable plans go in `docs/`.
- Structured claim and source records go in `records/`.
