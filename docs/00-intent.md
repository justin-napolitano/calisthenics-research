# Intent

## Purpose

Create a durable personal workspace for calisthenics practice, exercise research, daily logs, and routine planning.

## Immediate Goal

Seed a baseline bodyweight routine that can be done daily as a habit anchor while still respecting recovery. The first version should require little or no equipment, train the major movement patterns, and be simple enough to log consistently.

## Scope

In scope:

- Daily calisthenics logs.
- Research notes about bodyweight training, recovery, mobility, and progression.
- Baseline routines and changes over time.
- Personal observations about adherence, soreness, effort, and exercise response.
- Structured claim and source records.

Out of scope for now:

- Nutrition planning.
- Medical diagnosis or injury rehabilitation.
- Wearable integrations.
- Automated dashboards.
- Advanced skills such as handstands, muscle-ups, levers, or planches.

## Safety Boundary

The repo can help organize evidence and logs. It cannot decide whether a symptom is safe. Pain, dizziness, chest symptoms, faintness, or injury-specific questions should be treated as stop signals or clinician questions.
