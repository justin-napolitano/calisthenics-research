# SP-0005: Equilibrium Vocabulary

Status: draft
Parent: EP-0003

## Goal

Define the vocabulary needed before implementing movement records.

## Terms To Define

- balance
- equilibrium
- postural orientation
- base of support
- center of mass
- center of pressure
- perturbation
- anticipatory postural adjustment
- recovery strategy
- kuzushi
- tsukuri
- kake
- frame
- clinch
- immobilization
- escape
- pressure tier
- cooperative resistance
- live resistance

## Review Rule

No term becomes canonical until it has:

- a plain-language definition
- a source or explicit assumption marker
- at least one example
- at least one failure mode
