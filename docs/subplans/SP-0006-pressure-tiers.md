# SP-0006: Pressure Tiers

Status: draft
Parent: EP-0003

## Goal

Create a research-backed pressure model that distinguishes cooperative movement study from resistant self-defense claims.

## Draft Tiers

- Tier 0: avoidance, distance, and de-escalation.
- Tier 1: cooperative partner study.
- Tier 2: mild resistance.
- Tier 3: strong resistance.
- Tier 4: aggressive attack simulation under controlled rules.
- Tier 5: severe threat, outside normal training scope.

## Research Tasks

- Compare this model with judo cooperative versus competitive studies.
- Compare this model with ecological dynamics and representative learning design.
- Identify where Aikido assumptions hold and fail.
- Identify where grappling, boxing, and Muay Thai provide pressure-tested constraints.

## Safety Boundary

This repo should not publish high-pressure self-defense recommendations without qualified instruction, legal awareness, and explicit safety framing.
