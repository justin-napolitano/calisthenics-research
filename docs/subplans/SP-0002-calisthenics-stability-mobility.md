Status: active

## Goal

Use calisthenics to support health, joint durability, balance, mobility, and martial transfer. Hypertrophy and max strength are secondary.

The current thesis to test is documented in [Calisthenics Thesis](../research/calisthenics/2026-06-08-calisthenics-thesis.md).
