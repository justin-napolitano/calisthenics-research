# SP-0003: Martial Systems Research Plan

Status: draft
Parent: EP-0001

## Goal

Study grappling, boxing, and Muay Thai as movement systems that can be reduced to atoms, drills, constraints, and transfer links.

## Initial Domains

- Grappling: base, frames, posture, pressure, hip escape, bridge, sprawl.
- Boxing: stance, guard, jab, cross, hook, pivot, range.
- Muay Thai: stance, check, teep, round kick, knee, clinch, sweep base.

## Research Questions

- Which atoms are shared across all three martial domains?
- Which martial skills overlap with calisthenics, running, and yoga?
- Which terms are tradition labels versus physical explanations?
- What evidence can be used without overclaiming?

## Review Rule

Do not convert martial claims into recommendations until they are either sourced, coached, or logged as personal experiments.
