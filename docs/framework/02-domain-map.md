- cadence
- posture
- foot strike under center of mass
- breath rhythm
- sustainable intensity

### Calisthenics

- squat
- hinge
- push
- pull
- trunk control
- lateral stability
- balance

### Yoga And Mobility

Primary patterns:

- range control
- breath-led pacing

- recovery signal detection

## Cross-Domain Transfers

- Squat base transfers to grappling stance and defensive posture.
- Hip hinge transfers to shot entries, sprawls, and force absorption.
- Single-leg balance transfers to running, kicking, checking, and guard recovery.
- Trunk rotation transfers to punching, kicking, running stride, and throws.
- Frames transfer from scapular control and pushing strength into grappling defense and clinch.
- Breath control transfers from yoga into sustainable running and sparring composure.
