title: Logbook Model
description: The working shape of future health logs before app and database implementation.
---

The logbook is low priority until the protocol is stable, but the site should keep the model visible.

## Logging Goal

Capture enough signal to make better decisions without turning the journal into an administrative burden.

## Minimum Fields

| Field | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| Date | Anchor the practice in time. |
| Session type | Cardio, calisthenics, morning floor, hot yoga, equipment, supine, recovery. |
| Duration | Estimate total dose. |
| Intensity | Easy, moderate, hard, or specific RPE later. |
| Recovery note | Soreness, pain flags, energy, sleep, heat response. |
| Decision note | What to keep, reduce, increase, or research. |

## Later App Direction

An iOS app can eventually pull local Health data, allow fast manual logs, and push structured records to an active database. That is deferred until the site and active research projects are stable.
