Claims in this journal should be treated differently depending on their support.

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Supported | Backed by stable guideline or institutional evidence. |
| Hypothesis | A reasonable working belief that still needs testing. |
| Personal signal | Something logs suggest, not a general health claim. |
| Open question | Needs more research before it should drive the protocol. |

## Research Rule

New protocol claims should link to source notes or references before they are promoted into active routine pages.
