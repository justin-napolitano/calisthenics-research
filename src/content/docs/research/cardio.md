## Current Understanding

The initial cardio hypothesis is 180-240 moderate-equivalent minutes per week, built around a fixed Wednesday night 5K. The goal is to find the sustainable benefit-to-burden range, not to maximize running volume.

## Practical Rule

Keep one hard anchor. Fill the rest of the week with easy aerobic work unless logs show recovery is strong enough for more.

## Open Questions

- Does the Wednesday 5K stay sustainable when paired with Friday calisthenics?
- Does Saturday easy cardio add benefit or accumulate fatigue?
- How should hot yoga be counted, if at all, in cardio minutes?
