---
title: Cardio Dose For Heart Health
description: Research project for finding the sustainable cardio dose around a fixed Wednesday 5K.
---

## Thesis

Optimal cardio is the best sustainable benefit-to-burden range, not maximum running volume.

## Current Hypothesis

Start with 180-240 moderate-equivalent minutes per week around the fixed Wednesday night 5K.

## Protocol

- Wednesday night 5K as the only planned hard run.
- Tuesday easy aerobic work.
- Saturday easy run, walk-jog, bike, or long walk.
- Sunday recovery walk if useful.

## Metrics

| Metric | Reason |
| --- | --- |
| Moderate-equivalent minutes | Main weekly dose signal. |
| Vigorous minutes | Prevent accidental intensity creep. |
| Running minutes | Separate running stress from total cardio. |
| Lower-leg soreness | Primary overuse warning. |
| 5K effort | Keep the anchor sustainable. |

## Falsifiers

- Lower-leg pain repeats.
- The 5K turns into a weekly race.
- Fatigue trends up for two weeks.
- Adherence falls below 80 percent.
