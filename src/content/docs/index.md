This is a working research journal for health practice. It tracks what I am doing, what I currently believe, what evidence supports those beliefs, and what personal logs eventually prove or disprove.

The current phase is intentionally simple: establish the cardio, calisthenics, morning floor, hot yoga, and recovery baseline before building heavier database, iOS logging, or movement-graph features.

## Current Focus

| Area | Working thesis | Status |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cardio | Test 180-240 moderate-equivalent minutes per week around a fixed Wednesday 5K. | Active |
| Calisthenics | Two focused full-body sessions plus daily low-dose movement should cover the health baseline. | Active |
| Morning floor | A short daily floor routine should preserve mobility, trunk control, posture, and consistency without becoming another workout. | Active |
| Hot yoga | Thursday hot yoga is mobility, balance, breath, heat exposure, and recovery signal. | Testing |
